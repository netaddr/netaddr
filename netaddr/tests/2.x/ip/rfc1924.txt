The example from the RFC.

{{{

>>> from netaddr.ip.rfc1924 import ipv6_to_base85, base85_to_ipv6

>>> ip_addr = '1080::8:800:200c:417a'

>>> ip_addr
'1080::8:800:200c:417a'
    
>>> base85 = ipv6_to_base85(ip_addr)

>>> base85
'4)+k&C#VzJ4br>0wv%Yp'

>>> base85_to_ipv6(base85)
'1080::8:800:200c:417a'

}}}
